About Native American Resource Page

Native American resource is a 3rd party company that is licensed to sell to tribes, we help connect supplier and tribes. As a woman owned Native American owned company, we help serve businesses that need to meet their obligation to purchased from minority owned companies. We represent the supplier, to the tribe, in order to facilitate sales on their behalf.
